Notes

Compound Inequality Chain - has two inequality symbols with a variable in the middle
ex: -4 < x <= 1 means -4 < x and x <= 1
Solving a Compound Inequality Chain - if the variable is not isolated, perform the inverse operations to eliminate everything around the variable
Compound Inequality joined by "and" - Two simple inequalities joined by and can be combined into a compound inequality (may also need to solve)
Compound Inequality joined by "or" - Two simple inequalities joined by "or" cannot be combined into a chain
