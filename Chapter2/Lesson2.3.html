Notes

Finding the Solutions to System of Inequalities - You determine whether an ordered pair is in the solution set by substitution. Substitue the x and y coordinates of the ordered pair for the variable x and y in both inequalities and check if both inequalities are true.
Solving Systems of Inequalities Graphically - Graph both on he same graph and shade in. The overlap (including solid line) is the solution set.
