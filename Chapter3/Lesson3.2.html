Notes

Slope - represents how steep the line is
Positive Slope - (starting from left of graph) going upward
Negative Slope - (starting from left of graph) going downward
Slope of Zero - line is horizontal
No Slope - line is vertical
Finding the Slope Graphically - move from the left to right m = (rise/run)
Finding the Slope Algebraically - difference in y-values (rise) y2 - y1 over difference in x-values (run) x2 - x1
example: slope of a line that goes through points (1,3), (2,6) - 6-3/2-1 = 3/1 = 3
