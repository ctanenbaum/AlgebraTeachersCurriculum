Graphing an Equation in Slope-Intercept Form
  1. use the y-intercept to plot (0,b) on the y-axis
  2. use the slope to determine at least 2 more points
  3. draw a line through the points and label the line with the equation
Write an Equation Given Point and Slope
  1. solve for b by filling in your point by x and y
    ex: x = 1, y = -3, m = 2 equation: -3 = 2(1) + b
  2. Write in Point slope form: y - y1 = m(x - x1) and simplify until you get y=mx+b
